About Me


I am currently a freshman at the University of Technology and Economics in Budapest and my major is Industrial Design Engineering. Within the university I am a member of the Graphic Study Club, where I have got the possibility to be encountered with advanced studies like Visual Design, Media Design and Graphic Design.

In the following pictures you can see some of my previous works. In my high school years besides descriptive geometry and square reconstruction I have mostly done settings and portraits with charcoal. And at last but not least I have also dabbled a bit in logo and other kind of designs.
